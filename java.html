Why another Blog for Java?

This blog was started with a different approach towards Java, and that approach is of a Kid! who goes into questioning very fundamental things which are quite important and most of the times ignored or not emphasised upon, while learning Java. This Blog aims to question everything from small to complex concepts in Java.
