Attacks on RFID Technology

A technical report on attacks on RFID technology which touches upon the basics of RFID along with aspects of security and privacy vulnerabilities along with its design, architecture and cryptology of the technology that is used by almost every corporate, government and institution.
Status: First Version Completed
Team: 1

 

eCommerce Stencil

At SSS, an eCommerce portal has been built specifically for small-scale businesses and enterprises, which can be customised according to their requirements. Stencil stands firm on the technology stack consisting of AngularJS, Bootstrap Material Design, Spring MVC, MySQL; which makes it a one-of-its-kind eCommerce software.

HydroFlux

HydroFlux is an IoT project with the aim to completely automate the mundane routine of filling the water tank of a household or any complex by leveraging the power of Internet of Things. Raspberry Pi, Ionic mobile app and IBM's IoT server architecture are being used to accomplish the objective.
Status: Under Development
Team: 1
